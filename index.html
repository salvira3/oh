Welcome to Fasilkom!

Faculty of Computer Science Universitas Indonesia is one of the best Faculty in Indonesia that has a vision to maintain its position as an internationally acclaimed hub for research and education in the field of CS and IT in Indonesia. FASILKOM UI also strives to maintain its health and integrity as an organization, as well as to continue working towards Indonesia's advancement in these fields.

Open House Fasilkom 2017

#MakeITFun

Sabtu, 14 Oktober 2017

08.30 - selesai

Fakultas Ilmu Komputer UI, Depok, Jawa Barat

Open House Fasilkom 2017 is an annual event with a purpose to give publication for the Faculty of Computer Science. Through this event, seniors in high school will be able to know and get the idea of the university life in Fasilkom. Additionally, Open House participants will also get new knowledge about information technology work field, real product of information technology and non-academic activities in Fasilkom UI.
